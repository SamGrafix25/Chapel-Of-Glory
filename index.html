Christ In Us... Is The Hope Of Glory (Colossians 1:27).

Overview

At Chapel of Glory, our commitment to spiritual growth is firmly rooted in the teachings and values of the Holy Bible. We uphold the Bible as the ultimate guide for every aspect of life, making it the foundation of all spiritual learning and development within the institution.

The chapel is dedicated to directing hearts toward Omini Potent, fostering an atmosphere where He is worshipped sincerely and truthfully. It serves as a unifying space for the University community—bringing together staff, students, and visitors for worship, spiritual growth, and meaningful engagement on life-related matters.

At Chapel of Glory, In Thomas Adewum University, our mission is centered on placing God above all by nurturing a deep and personal relationship with Him, rooted in scriptural truth (Luke 10:27; John 1:12). We are committed to delivering sound, thought-provoking biblical teachings designed not just to inform, but to transform lives through the active work of the Holy Spirit (2 Timothy 3:16–17).

About COG Chapel
