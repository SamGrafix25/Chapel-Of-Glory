Our Vision

To provide a Christ-centered environment that fosters spiritual growth, academic excellence, and character development through dynamic worship, biblical teaching, and compassionate service to the university community and beyond.

Benediction

In chapel of Glory, we're empowered and strengthened by prayer, we're rooted in the love of Christ, Christ's excellence is reflected in all we do, through us Jesus is seen, Jesus is revealed and Jesus is glorified. Christ in us is the hope of glory

  • Build godly character
  • Support academic excellence
  • Foster unity and love
